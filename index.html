Who we are?

Afro++ (Afroplusplus) is a Tech Start-up based in Johannesburg, South Africa. We offer software development services more specifically web applications. We aim to improve technology in South Africa and expand all across the continent. The internet exists to make information accessible more convienently and our duty is to build platforms that make it possible.

What we do?

We develop web applications of different kinds based on our clients needs. Our team of talented developers is dedicated to using the latest technologies to push the production to its limits. We make professional elegant designs that are appealing to your target market. We oftenly target small businesses and start-ups who do not already have a tech division in their companies.

Why choose us?

Our plan is to make online presence affordable and competitive for everyone no matter how small your business is. The idea is to help our local entrepreneurs grow by connecting to as many people as possible. Thus choosing us gives you the ability to attract customers to see you just as valuable and professional as large scale businesses.
